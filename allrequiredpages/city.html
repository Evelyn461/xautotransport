Frequently Asked Questions

How long does auto transport take across the country?

Typically 7-14 days, depending on distance and route; we provide exact timelines based on origin and destination.

Is enclosed transport better for luxury cars?

Yes, it protects from weather and debris; open is fine for standard vehicles but exposes to elements.

Do I need to prepare my car for shipping?

Remove personal items, check fluids, and ensure it's drivable; we inspect for cleanliness and condition.

What if my vehicle arrives damaged?

Our carriers have cargo insurance; file claims with photos we assist with paperwork and repairs.

How do door-to-door services work?

We pick up from your address and deliver to the final spot, adjusting for access like garages.

What's the difference between brokers and carriers?

Brokers connect you to transporters; we handle both for direct control and pricing.
